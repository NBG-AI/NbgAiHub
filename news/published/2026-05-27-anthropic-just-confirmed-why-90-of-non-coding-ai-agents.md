Anthropic analyzed millions of AI agent tool calls and found that about 50% of agent activity is in software engineering, with much less in other business areas like sales or finance. The key insight is that AI agents perform well in coding because of clean, structured data, but struggle in non-coding domains due to messy, unstructured real-world business data, highlighting a major challenge for enterprise AI deployments.
